# Second Brain

> **Area Context:** This serves as the Map of Content (MOC) and strategic hub for the Second Brain area.

## 🎯 The Goal
A continuously operating, high-signal, self-pruning personal knowledge management system powered by AI agents (Claude and Antigravity) that accelerates execution.

## 💡 The Idea & Scope
Maintaining, evolving, and governing the agentic Second Brain infrastructure and methodologies.

**In Scope:** AI agent workflows, prompt tuning, system documentation (`99-system`), automation scripts, UI/Obsidian configuration.
**Out of Scope:** Actual domain-specific content (e.g., project notes for work or hobbies). This area is strictly for the *meta-system* of the brain itself.

## ⚙️ How (The Pipeline / Strategy)
Curating ideas/tools into drafts, testing new AI skills safely, peer-reviewing them via the `agent-kanban.md`, and documenting changes in `second-brain-operating-system.md` before deploying.

# Marketing Strategy & Brand Positioning — StandupZero

> Phase-6 deliverable. Draws from `02-prd.md` + [[16-usp]], [[03-customer-discovery]], [[07-gtm-strategy]]. Depth 3: positioning, messaging, content engine, the honest brand problem.

## Summary
Marketing must do the heavy lifting that differentiation can't: with a *vague* USP ([[16-usp]]) and a name collision ([[05-legal-scan]]), the brand can't out-claim rivals, so it competes on **credibility and developer-native voice**, channeled through the OSS CLI. The defining move: lead with a *demonstration* ("here's your standup, built from your last 5 commits"), not a *claim*.

## Brand positioning
- **Category:** async standup tool (a known category — don't invent one; [[07-gtm-strategy]]).
- **Positioning line (working):** *"Your standup, written from your work."* True, concrete, developer-native. Honest limit: rivals can say similar ([[16-usp]]) — so we win on *proof*, not slogan.
- **Voice:** by-developers-for-developers; technical, no-fluff, privacy-respecting. The anti-"engagement-bot" tone.
- **⚠ Brand risk:** "StandupZero" vs "ZeroStandup" creates SEO/recognition confusion and possible trademark exposure ([[05-legal-scan]]). **Resolve the name before any brand investment** — a marketing strategy built on a contested name compounds the problem.

## Messaging by audience ([[03-customer-discovery]])
- **Engineer:** "Stop typing your standup. It's drafted from your commits — review and post in 5 seconds."
- **Eng manager:** "Status you can trust, because it's built from real work — not from memory at 9am."
- **Privacy-conscious team:** "Metadata only. Your code is never read or stored." (also seeds the [[12-blue-ocean]] angle.)

## Content & demand engine (near-zero budget)
1. **Show, don't tell:** an interactive demo / the OSS CLI itself — let developers generate a real draft in 60 seconds.
2. **Comparison & technical content:** "Geekbot/Standuply alternative" SEO; "how we infer 'today' from git activity" engineering posts (credibility + organic).
3. **Community presence:** HN launch, Rands/r/ExperiencedDevs/LeadDev, dev X — the [[03-customer-discovery]] watering holes, not paid ads (uneconomic, [[07-gtm-strategy]]).
4. **Design-partner case studies** → social proof → referral loop.

## Trade-offs & alternatives
- **Demonstration-led (chosen)** vs **claim-led campaigns** — claims are commoditized and unaffordable; a working demo is the only credible, cheap differentiator-substitute.
- **Own-category creation (rejected)** — fighting to define a new category as a solo founder is too expensive; ride the existing "async standup" search intent.
- **Privacy as a secondary message (chosen now)** — kept as a supporting message under the head-on plan, but it's the ready-made primary message if a pivot to [[12-blue-ocean]] is triggered.

## Open questions & dependencies
- **Name resolution is a blocker to brand spend** ([[05-legal-scan]]).
- Which message converts — time-saving vs trust vs privacy? A/B on the landing page ([[05-kpi-framework]]).
- Content cadence is founder-time-bound (the scarce resource, [[01-business-model-design]]).

# Job Hunt

> **Area hub (MOC).** A personal job-hunt run powered by the `job-hunt-*` skills plus **[career-ops](https://github.com/santifer/career-ops)** as the execution hub. Full usage guide: `vault/99-system/documentation/job-hunt-toolset.md`.

## 🎯 The Goal
Run a real job hunt on a toolset you control: build a CV-as-data spine, a story bank, interview prep, and company dossiers in the vault; adopt **career-ops** to find, deep-score, and apply to roles. The vault makes *you* sharp; career-ops is the engine.

## 🧰 The Toolset
**Build (vault skills, `job-hunt-*`):** `cv-from-doc` (CV → `cv.md` spine) · `craft-profile` (portfolio HTML) · `capture-story` (STAR diary) · `interview-prep` · `mock-interview` · `scout-company` (dossier + me-vs) · `interview-debrief` · `careerops-profile` / `careerops-portals` (configure career-ops) · `starter` (progress dashboard).

**Adopt (outside the vault):** **career-ops** — deep A–F evaluation, ATS CV PDF, cover letters, company research, outreach, application tracking. Your `cv.md` is symlinked into it.

## 🗂️ Structure
- **`hunter/`** — your operational data (private): `personal-profile/` (CV + portfolio), `career/` (stories + prep), `scouted-companies/` (per-company dossiers + debriefs).
- **`draft/` · `scout/` · `synthesis/`** — the area's thinking layer (research, discovery, strategy).
- **[[todo-kanban]]** — the area's task board · **[[to-check]]** — triage queue.

## 🔒 Privacy
Everything under `hunter/` is **personal data** — keep it in your own private remote, never publish it. This template ships the skills + an empty structure only.

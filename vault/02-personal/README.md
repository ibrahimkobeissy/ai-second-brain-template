# 02-personal

The **Personal** folder is dedicated to your personal life, hobbies, and areas of interest that are not tied to your professional work.

## Philosophy
- **Strict Separation**: Keep personal notes isolated from professional ones.
- **Area Hubs**: Organize your personal goals, life management, and interests into distinct Areas.
- **AI Processing**: Just like in the `01-work` folder, you can use the AI agents to synthesize notes and manage kanban tasks for your personal projects.

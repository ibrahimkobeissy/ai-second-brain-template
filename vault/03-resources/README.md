# 03-resources

The **Resources** folder acts as your reference library. It contains topics of interest, general knowledge, and reference material that are not directly tied to a specific active Area in Work or Personal.

## Philosophy
- **Evergreen Knowledge**: Store durable, long-term knowledge here.
- **Cross-Domain Linking**: Connect resources to your active areas using `[[wikilinks]]`. 
- **High-Signal**: Ensure the notes here are well-curated and not just a dumping ground for raw bookmarks (which belong in the Inbox).

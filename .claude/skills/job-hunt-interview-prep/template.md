# Interview Prep

## Behavioral / HR
1. Tell me about yourself.
2. Why this role / why us?
3. Your greatest strength — with proof.
4. A real weakness — and what you do about it.
5. Your proudest achievement.
6. A time you failed — and what changed after.
7. A conflict with a colleague or manager — how you handled it.
8. A time you led without authority.
9. A time you worked under heavy pressure / a tight deadline.
10. A time you had to handle ambiguity or a sudden change.
11. Where do you want to be in 3–5 years?
12. Why are you leaving your current role?

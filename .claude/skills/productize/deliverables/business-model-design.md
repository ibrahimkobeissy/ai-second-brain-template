# Business Model Design — deliverable spec

**Generates:** the recommended revenue + delivery model (SaaS / freemium / usage-based / marketplace / licensing / hybrid).
**Reads:** PRD 1.4 + `financial-model` + `value-proposition` + `market-study`.
**Method:** weigh candidate models against the segment and unit economics; pick one with rationale, name the runner-up and why not.

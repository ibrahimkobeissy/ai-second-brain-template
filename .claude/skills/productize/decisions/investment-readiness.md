# Investment Readiness — decision spec

**Evaluates:** whether the business case is strong enough to attract funding or justify self-investment.
**Reads:** `financial-model`, `market-study`, `feasibility`, `risk-assessment`.
**Method:**
- Check the case an investor probes: market size, unit economics, traction/evidence, team, and the risk register.
- Name what's **missing** to be fundable (the gating evidence), not just what's present.
**Writes:** a decision artifact per `templates/decision.md`. `recommendation` reframed as ready / not-yet / no. Cite the gaps.

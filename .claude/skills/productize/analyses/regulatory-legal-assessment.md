# Regulatory & Legal Assessment — analysis spec

**Evaluates:** licensing, compliance, tax and legal barriers in the target sector/geography (the broad pass; `legal-scan` does the detailed 7-area scan).
**Reads:** PRD 1.6 (geography/regulatory), 1.3 (offering).
**Method:**
- Identify required licenses/registrations to operate legally.
- Name the compliance regimes that apply (and roughly what they demand).
- Flag where specialist legal advice is genuinely needed.
**Writes:** artifact per template. `verdict` ∈ {low-barrier, manageable, heavy}. `key_findings`: the binding requirement + whether it's a blocker.

> Informational only — **not legal advice.** Escalate real exposure to a qualified professional.

# Industry Analysis — analysis spec

**Evaluates:** structural attractiveness of the industry, not just direct rivals.
**Reads:** PRD 1.3 (category), 1.6 (geography/regulatory).
**Method:**
- Porter's Five Forces — entry barriers, supplier power, buyer power, substitutes, rivalry; rate each high/med/low **with a reason**.
- PESTEL scan for the macro forces that actually move this industry (skip the inapplicable ones; don't pad).
**Writes:** artifact per `templates/analysis.md`. `verdict` ∈ {attractive, mixed, hostile}. `key_findings`: dominant force(s) · the structural risk.

# Due Diligence — analysis spec

**Evaluates:** the financial, legal, operational and commercial reality behind a business or partnership.
**Reads:** PRD 1.7 (team/funding), plus any counterparty the user names.
**Method:**
- Verify claims against evidence — what's asserted vs. what's documented.
- Flag the gaps a buyer/partner/investor would probe (financial holes, legal exposure, key-person risk).
**Writes:** artifact per template. `verdict` ∈ {clean, caveats, red-flags}. `key_findings`: the material findings + anything that would block a deal.

# Customer Discovery & Persona Development — analysis spec

**Evaluates:** who the ideal customer really is, via research/interviews/behavioral data.
**Reads:** PRD 1.2 (initial segments/personas).
**Method:**
- Turn PRD 1.2's proto-personas into validated ICPs — or correct them where evidence disagrees.
- Capture the job-to-be-done, the buying trigger, and where these people actually congregate.
- Prep the interview guide (Mom Test style — past behavior, not hypotheticals).
**Writes:** artifact per template. `verdict` ∈ {clear-ICP, partial, unclear}. `key_findings`: the sharpened ICP + the buying trigger.
